Child Safety Policy

Effective Date: 31-03-2025

1. Introduction

At Heatic Debate App, we are committed to providing a safe and secure online environment for all users, especially minors.

2. Safety Standards

  • Proactive Moderation: AI-driven content moderation and human review.
  • Strict User Policies: No CSAE content; strict actions against violations.
  • Age Verification: Ensuring minors do not access inappropriate content.
  • User Reporting: In-app reporting for concerns.
  • Community Guidelines Enforcement: Active monitoring and removal of harmful content.

3. Reporting and Response

  • In-App Reporting: "Report" button within the app.
  • Support Email: heaticdeveloper@gmail.com
  • Law Enforcement Collaboration: We report all verified cases as required.

4. Prevention and Compliance

  • COPPA: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act.
  • GDPR-K: General Data Protection Regulation for Kids.
  • NCMEC: National Center for Missing & Exploited Children Reporting.

5. Contact Information

Email: heaticdeveloper@gmail.com

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes.
